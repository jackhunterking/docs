Lovable is an AI-powered app builder that lets you create web applications using natural language. This guide shows you how to connect AdsGateway to Lovable via its Custom MCP connector.

<Info>
  Lovable supports MCP through its **Connectors** feature. You can add AdsGateway as a custom MCP server to query your ad data directly from the Lovable chat.
</Info>

## Prerequisites

- A [Lovable](https://lovable.dev) account with an active project
- An [AdsGateway account](https://adsgateway.io)

## Setup Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Connectors">
    In your Lovable project, click the **+** icon at the bottom of the chat panel. In the menu that appears, find **Connectors** and click **Manage connectors**.

    <img src="/images/step-1.png" alt="Open Connectors menu in Lovable" style={{ borderRadius: '8px', border: '1px solid #333', marginTop: '12px' }} />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Custom Connector">
    On the **Connectors** page, scroll down to the **Personal connectors** section and click **Custom** â€” "Connect your own MCP".

    <img src="/images/step-2.png" alt="Select Custom connector under Personal connectors" style={{ borderRadius: '8px', border: '1px solid #333', marginTop: '12px' }} />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure the MCP Server">
    In the **Add MCP server** dialog, enter the following details:

    | Field | Value |
    |-------|-------|
    | **Server name** | `Ads Gateway` |
    | **Server URL** | `https://ads-gateway.onrender.com/mcp` |
    | **Authentication** | **OAuth (default)** |

    Then click **Add & authorize**.

    <img src="/images/step-3.png" alt="Add MCP server dialog with Ads Gateway configuration" style={{ borderRadius: '8px', border: '1px solid #333', marginTop: '12px' }} />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign In and Authorize">
    A browser window will open for authentication:

    1. **Sign in** to your AdsGateway account (or click "Sign up" to create one)
    2. Once authenticated, you will see **"Authorization successful!"**
    3. You will be automatically redirected back to Lovable

    <img src="/images/step-4.png" alt="AdsGateway sign in and authorization successful screens" style={{ borderRadius: '8px', border: '1px solid #333', marginTop: '12px' }} />
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  You are now connected! AdsGateway tools are available in your Lovable chat.
</Check>

## Verify the Connection

Once connected, try asking this in the Lovable chat:

```
List my Meta ad accounts
```

You should see a list of your connected ad accounts.

## Available Commands

After setup, you can ask Lovable questions like:

<AccordionGroup>

</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Authentication window doesn't open">
    1. Check if pop-ups are blocked in your browser
    2. Try manually visiting [adsgateway.io](https://adsgateway.io) and logging in first
    3. Then remove the connector and re-add it
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="'Authorization failed' error">
    1. Make sure you are using the correct AdsGateway credentials
    2. Try clearing your browser cookies for adsgateway.io
    3. Re-add the connector and authorize again
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="No tools available after connecting">
    1. Make sure the connector shows as connected in your Connectors page
    2. Try refreshing the Lovable page
    3. Remove and re-add the connector if the issue persists
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Connection lost or expired">
    Your OAuth session may have expired. Remove the Ads Gateway connector from Personal connectors and add it again following the setup steps above.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
