---
title: "Quickstart"
---

Follow these steps to connect your advertising accounts to your favorite AI assistant in just 5 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an AdsGateway Account">
    Go to [adsgateway.io](https://adsgateway.io) and sign up for a free account.
    
    <Tip>
      You can sign up with Google or create an account with your email.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect Your AI Assistant">
    Choose your AI tool and follow the setup guide:
    
    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Cursor" icon="code" href="/ai-integrations/cursor">
        One-click installation for developers
      </Card>
      <Card title="Claude" icon="message-bot" href="/ai-integrations/claude">
        Claude Code CLI or Claude Desktop
      </Card>
      <Card title="Windsurf" icon="wind" href="/ai-integrations/windsurf">
        AI development environment
      </Card>
      <Card title="ChatGPT" icon="sparkles" href="/ai-integrations/chatgpt">
        Coming soon
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect Your Ad Platform">
    After setting up your AI assistant, connect your advertising account:
    
    1. Open your AI assistant
    2. It will prompt you to authenticate with AdsGateway
    3. Click the link to authorize access
    4. Connect your Meta Ads account (more platforms coming soon)
    
    <Info>
      AdsGateway uses OAuth for secure authentication. We never store your ad platform credentials.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Your Ad Account">
    Once connected, ask your AI assistant:
    
    ```
    List my Meta ad accounts
    ```
    
    Then select the account you want to work with:
    
    ```
    Select the account "My Business Account"
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Asking Questions">
    You're all set! Try these example prompts:
    
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Performance Overview">
        ```
        Give me a summary of my ad account performance for the last 7 days
        ```
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Campaign List">
        ```
        Show me all my active campaigns
        ```
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Anomaly Detection">
        ```
        Are there any anomalies in my ad performance?
        ```
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Top Performers">
        ```
        Which campaigns had the biggest changes this week?
        ```
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>
</Steps>
