## What You Can Do

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Ask questions in natural language">
    Instead of navigating complex dashboards, simply ask:
    - "What was my ad spend yesterday?"
    - "Which campaigns are performing best this week?"
    - "Show me any anomalies in my Meta ads"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Get AI-powered analysis">
    AdsGateway provides intelligent analysis tools:
    - **Account summaries** with key metrics and trends
    - **Top movers** showing biggest performance changes
    - **Anomaly detection** to catch issues early
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Work in your favorite AI tool">
    Use AdsGateway with:
    - **Cursor** - AI-powered code editor
    - **Claude Desktop** - Anthropic's AI assistant
    - **ChatGPT** - OpenAI's conversational AI
    - **Windsurf** - AI development environment
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Example Conversation

Here's what working with AdsGateway looks like:

<Steps>
  <Step title="You ask a question">
    "Show me my Meta ad account performance for the last 7 days"
  </Step>
  <Step title="AdsGateway fetches your data">
    The AI connects to your Meta Ads account via AdsGateway and retrieves the metrics
  </Step>
  <Step title="You get actionable insights">
    "Your account spent $2,450 with 125,000 impressions. CTR is 2.3%, up 15% from last week. Your 'Summer Sale' campaign is your top performer with a 3.2x ROAS."
  </Step>
</Steps>
